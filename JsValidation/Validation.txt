-----Javascript Form Validation------

- It is important to validate the  form submitted by the user to check if it is appropriate or not.
- With the javascript validation on the client side we can make our form validation more efficient it it will take less time than that of the server-side validation.

------Javascript Email Validation--------

There are many criteria that need to followed while email validation

-email id must contain the @ and . character
-There must be at least one character before and after @
- There must be at least two characters after the .
